# Let Us Believe in the Beginning of the Cold Season

*Forough Farrokhzad.*

And this is me
a woman alone

at the beginning of grasping the earth's polluted being
and the plain, sorrowful despair of the sky
and the helplessness of these hands of cement.

I am cold
I am cold, and it seems I shall never be warm again

time has such a weight
and how the fishes chew at my flesh
why do you keep me always at the bottom of the sea?

I will let go of the lines
and likewise I will let go of the counting of numbers

I will take refuge in the open expanses of feeling
I am naked, naked, naked

and my wounds are all from love

I have carried this wandering island
through the revolution of the ocean
and the explosion of the mountain

راز منوری است که آنرا
آن آخرین و آن کشیده‌ترین شعله خوب می‌داند.

Hello, O strangeness of loneliness
I surrender the room to you
because the dark clouds are always
the prophets of newly purifying verses
and in the martyrdom of a candle
there is a luminous secret that
the last and the most drawn-out flame knows well.

ایمان بیاوریم
ایمان بیاوریم به آغاز فصل سرد
ایمان بیاوریم به ویرانه‌های باغ‌های تخیل
به داس‌های واژگون‌شده‌ی بیکار
و دانه‌های زندانی.
نگاه کن که چه برفی می‌بارد …

Let us believe
let us believe in the beginning of the cold season
let us believe in the ruins of the gardens of imagination
in the overturned, idle sickles
and the imprisoned seeds.
look how the snow is falling …

شاید حقیقت آن دو دست جوان بود، آن دو دست جوان
که زیر بارش یکریز برف مدفون شد
و سال دیگر، وقتی بهار
با آسمان پشت پنجره همخوابه می‌شود
و در تنش فوران می‌کنند
فواره‌های سبز ساقه‌های سبکبار
شکوفه خواهدداد ای یار، ای یگانه‌ترین یار

Perhaps the truth was those two young hands, those two young hands
that were buried under the ceaseless falling of the snow
and next year, when spring
lies with the sky behind the window
and in its body there gush
the green fountains of the light-burdened stems
it will blossom, O love, O most singular love

ایمان بیاوریم به آغاز فصل سرد …

Let us believe in the beginning of the cold season …
